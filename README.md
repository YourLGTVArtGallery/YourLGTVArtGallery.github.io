# TV Art

Your personal photo library as a fullscreen ambient art gallery on your LG smart TV.

**[yourlgtvartgallery.github.io](https://yourlgtvartgallery.github.io)**

## Why we built this

We wanted our TV to double as an art frame, cycling through our own photos instead of a generic screensaver. We looked for an app that did that. We couldn't find one, so we built it.

TV Art started as a project for our own living room. We're now sharing it with anyone who wants the same thing: free, no accounts, no subscriptions, no catch.

It's a passion project, built and maintained in our spare time.

## Features

- **Zero subscriptions** — uses your existing OneDrive or Mylio Photos+ library. No new accounts, no monthly fees.
- **Ambient display** — fullscreen crossfade slideshow with an optional Ken Burns effect. Stays on during your set hours.
- **Remote friendly** — designed for the LG Magic Remote. Skip, pause, hide an image, or open settings without a keyboard.
- **Private by default** — no backend server. Images are fetched directly from your storage provider to your TV.

Works with Microsoft OneDrive, Mylio Photos+, and Google Drive (in progress).

## Get TV Art
