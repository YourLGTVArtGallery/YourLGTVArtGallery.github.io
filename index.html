TV Art

Your personal photo library as a fullscreen ambient art gallery on your LG smart TV.

Report a bug or request a feature Privacy Policy

Features

Zero subscriptions

Uses your existing OneDrive or Mylio Photos+ library. No new accounts, no monthly fees.

Ambient display

Fullscreen crossfade slideshow with optional Ken Burns effect. Stays on during your set hours.

Remote friendly

Designed for the LG Magic Remote. Skip, pause, hide an image, or open settings without a keyboard.

Private by default

No backend server. Images are fetched directly from your storage provider to your TV.

Works with

Microsoft OneDrive Mylio Photos+ Google Drive

Why we built this

We wanted our TV to double as an art frame, cycling through our own photos instead of a generic screensaver. We looked for an app that did that. We couldn't find one, so we built it.

TV Art started as a project for our own living room. We're now sharing it with anyone who wants the same thing: free, no accounts, no subscriptions, no catch.

It's a passion project, built and maintained in our spare time. Run into a bug, or have an idea for a feature? Tell us and we'll do our best to get to it. Want to help build it? We'd love that too.
