Support

Get help setting up or using TV Art on your LG TV.

Common questions

How do I install TV Art?

TV Art is available on the LG Content Store. Search for "TV Art" on your LG TV. You can also sideload it — see the GitHub repo for instructions.

Which storage providers are supported?

TV Art currently supports Microsoft OneDrive and Mylio Photos+. Google Drive support is in progress.

How do I connect OneDrive?

Select OneDrive on the provider screen. The app will show a code — visit the URL on your phone or computer, sign in with your Microsoft account, and enter the code. The TV connects automatically once approved.

How do I connect Mylio Photos+?

In the Mylio app, open an album, tap Share, and copy the link. On your TV, select Mylio Photos+ and paste the album code (the part after share.mylio.com/).

The slideshow stopped — what happened?

If active hours are set in Settings, the slideshow pauses outside those hours. You can also check that your TV is connected to the internet and your storage provider account is still connected.

How do I disconnect my account?

During the slideshow, press OK to open Settings. Scroll to the Library section and tap Disconnect. This removes all stored tokens from the TV.

Does TV Art store my photos anywhere?

No. Images are fetched directly from your storage provider to your TV. Nothing is stored on any server. See the Privacy Policy for full details.
