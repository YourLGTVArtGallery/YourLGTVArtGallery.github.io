# Contributing to TV Art

Thanks for wanting to help — TV Art is a passion project maintained in spare time, and contributions are welcome.

If you'd like to help build TV Art, email us at [support@xor0game.com](mailto:support@xor0game.com) and tell us what you're interested in working on. We'll get back to you and figure out where you can jump in.

For bugs and feature ideas, please use the [issue templates](https://github.com/YourLGTVArtGallery/YourLGTVArtGallery.github.io/issues/new/choose) instead of email.
